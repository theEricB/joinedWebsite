Latent Mies and other stories

A custom dataset is trained with 400 parametrically generated variants of four well-known modernist architectures. This dataset is then used to train a graph variational autoencoder. After training, the model generates new, unlimited, three-dimensional spatial configurations of buildings by sampling the latent space of the autoencoder.
